## Experiment Design

**Duration:** 4 weeks
**Scope:** 500 creators (randomized A/B test)
**Regions:** US, UK, France (English + French support)

### Test Groups

| Group | Features | Size |
|-------|----------|------|
| Control | Current TikTok Battle experience | 250 creators |
| Treatment | AI Battle Agents + Achievements | 250 creators |

### Primary Metrics

- **Revenue per Battle** (target: +40%)
- **Session Duration** (target: +60%)
- **Viewer Retention D7** (target: +25%)
- **Creator Satisfaction** (NPS survey)
