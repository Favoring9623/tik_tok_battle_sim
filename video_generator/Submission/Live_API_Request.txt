Subject: Live API Access Request - TikTok Battle Simulator

Dear TikTok Developer Relations Team,

We are requesting access to the Live API for our application "TikTok Battle Simulator".

--- APPLICATION OVERVIEW ---

TikTok Battle Simulator is a real-time competitive platform that gamifies TikTok Live streams by tracking gifts and creating interactive battles between creators. Our platform enhances creator engagement, increases viewer participation, and drives monetization through gift-based competitions.

Website: https://orionlabs.live
Demo Video: Available upon request

--- REQUESTED SCOPES ---

1. live.room.info
   Purpose: Detect if TikTok creators are currently live streaming and retrieve their room IDs to establish connections.
   Use Case: Users enter two creator usernames, and our platform checks their live status to enable real-time battle tracking.

2. live.gift.info
   Purpose: Track gift events in real-time during live battles to calculate scores and display analytics.
   Use Case:
   - Calculate battle scores based on gift coin values
   - Display real-time gift animations and notifications
   - Build leaderboards of top gifters
   - Generate post-battle analytics and replays

--- HOW IT BENEFITS THE TIKTOK ECOSYSTEM ---

For Creators:
- Increases engagement during live streams through gamification
- Drives gift-based monetization with competitive battles
- Provides analytics to understand gifting patterns
- OBS integration for professional streaming

For Viewers:
- Interactive participation through gift-based voting
- Real-time leaderboards and recognition
- Enhanced entertainment value during lives

For TikTok:
- Increases time spent on live streams
- Drives gift purchases and platform revenue
- Creates unique content opportunities
- Strengthens creator-viewer relationships

--- TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION ---

- OAuth 2.0 authentication via Login Kit
- WebSocket connections for real-time gift tracking
- Secure data handling (no financial data stored)
- Privacy-compliant design (GDPR ready)

--- DATA PRIVACY ---

We only access:
- Room status and IDs (live.room.info)
- Gift events: gift name, coin value, sender username (live.gift.info)

We do NOT access or store:
- Personal financial information
- Private messages
- Follower/following lists
- Access tokens (session-only)
